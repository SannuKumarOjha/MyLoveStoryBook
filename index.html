MY LOVE STORY !

This is the story of my first year of college, when I went to college, just 15 days after I went to college, the date of that day was 10th October, I fell in love with a girl, we can call it one-sided love.

MY LOVE STORY !

I started liking that girl but I didn't know whether she liked me or not and I didn't even try to find out.

Then one day I talked to that girl in the lab and she was attracting me with her words.

MY LOVE STORY !

Then I tried to talk to her on Snapchat on the pretext of asking for notes, then I came to know that she already had a boyfriend and that boy was from our college.

Then I thought that my love is one sided, let it remain one sided.

Then that boy abused me a lot on the phone and I did not like the abuses.

MY LOVE STORY !

Then that boy's luck opened, then I hit him not once but many times and even today I hit him.

MY LOVE STORY !

I was about to leave both of their lives, when he made a big mistake by abusing me.

After killing, that girl is still famous by my name in the entire college.

Now no matter how much I want to forget him, I don't understand how to forget him. No matter how much I tried to forget him, I still could not forget him.

THE END!
